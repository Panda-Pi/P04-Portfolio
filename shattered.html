shattered

Shattered is a kinectic sculpture that gives the perception of shattering glass when moving to discourage users from wasting energy in the building. The poster is meant to show what our sculpture does along with different configurations we have created. The poster also shows the exploded view of each configurations and the parts we have used to create our kinectic sculpture "Shattered". I have also created a short cinematographic video showing the movements of the sculpture.

Collaborators: Winnie Ku & Flora Wu
