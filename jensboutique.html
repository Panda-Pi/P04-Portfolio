jen's boutique

Jen's Boutique is a company that resells the branded products imported from the United States to Taiwanese residents that wants oversea products that are not sold in Taiwan. This is a template website for Jen's Boutique, therefore some links does not work. I designed the style of the website. We went for a simple and elegant look to attract people from different age groups.

visit site

This is the home page of the website. The first thing the customer would see when they visit the site would be the recent promotions and information of what the comapany is about. The customer can choose to shop either by promotion or by navigating through the menu to browse.

This browsing page where customers can see the selection of items all together. The design was kept simple to allow the items to stand out.

The detail page shows the customers the details of the item they choose. It also gives the customer view different views of the item along with a selection of different colours in which the item has. If the customer chooses this item, then they can simply choose choose the quantity click "add to bag"

The customer would proceed to their shopping bag. They can choose to edit their shopping bag such as changing the quantity of the item or removing an item they do not wish to have. The customer also has to choice to leave a feedback in the comment box below.

Once everything is all checked, the customer can proceed to checkout to purchase their item(s).

To purchase the item(s), the customer would either have to log in to their account or register if they are new to the site.
